Willam Shakespeare

  • 1564 - Born in Stratford-Upon-Avon, Warwickshire, England.
  • 1582 - Marries Anne Hathway.
  • 1583 - Birth of his first born Susanna.
  • 1585 - After the birth of his twins, Judith and Hamnet, Shakespeare left his family for London.
  • 1592 - It isn't 1592 that we begin to see records of Shakespeare's plays being performed on the London stage.
  • 1594 - The Lord Chamberlain's Men was the only company performing Shakespeare's plays. The Lord Chamberlain's Men were the leading play company in London.
  • 1599 - Shakespeare's play compnay built their own theatre which they named the Glode.
  • 1603 - The Lord Chamberlain's Men were awarded a royal patent by King James I and changed their name to the King's Men.
  • 1616- Shakespeare died one month after signing his well while claiming to be in perfect health.
